GPC Newsroom

The Challenge

Allow Georgia Perimeter College administrators to moderate and publish tons of news articles. Build the site on an existing Drupal 7 multisite platform.

The Work

Used HTML, CSS and PHP to build a responsive Drupal theme compatible with IE8. Constructed all content types, taxonomy terms, blocks and Views. Implemented custom sorting to facilitate site administration.

Also provided on-site training to administrators with varying levels of technical experience.

Under the direction of Morrison Agency.
