Georgia Perimeter College

The Challenge

Redesign the GPC Admissions homepage. Build a modern, responsive, HTML5 theme on an existing Drupal 6 multisite platform.

The Work

The number of existing responsive themes for Drupal 6 were few and cumbersome! So we decided to build a custom theme from scratch. The homepage (really three pages - Start, Explore, Apply) features a Drupal administered contact form, scheduled jumbotrons and a Google Map powered by the Views module.

Paid particular attention to removing extraneous markup from the PHP template files for lighter fields, nodes and views.

Under the direction of Morrison Agency.
