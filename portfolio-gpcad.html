Georgia Perimeter College

The Challenge

Redesign the GPC Admissions homepage. Provide a modern, responsive, html5 site on an existing drupal 6 multisite platform.

The Work

The number of existing responsive themes for Drupal 6 were few and cumbersome! So we decided to build a custom theme from scratch. The homepage (really three pages - Start, Explore, Apply) features a Drupal administered contact form, multi-day scheduled slideshow and a Google Maps JSON object using the Views module. Much attention was given to removing extraneous markup from the template files for lighter fields, nodes and views. Under the direction of Morrison Agency.
