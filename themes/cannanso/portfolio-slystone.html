Sly and The Family Stone

The Challenge

Add a Spotlight carousel feature to a Drupal base theme. The theme is used by hundreds of "turnkey" Sony Music artist websites including Sly and The Family Stone.

The Work

Wrote PHP scripts adding a carousel image content type to the Drupal install profile. The content type includes fields for an image, caption and call to action link. Matched the color styles dynamically with defined site values.

Under the direction of relentlessGENERATOR digital agency.
