NaturalHair Tube

The Challenge

Thinkful assignment to design and build a YouTube video search page. The results page displays all matches. Thumbnails link to their video on YouTube.

The Work

One of my favorite hobbies is watching YouTube videos from the Natural Hair Community. This fun project involved designing a responsive layout, writing semantic HTML, and obtaining a Google API key. Also added links to the YouTube channel. The most difficult task was setting the request page tokens when the Next and Previous buttons are clicked.
